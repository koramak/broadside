HOW IT WORKS · Drag from one of your blobs (or the RESERVE below your hull) and release on a deck section. Each drag sends a squad of ~10. Reserve goes ALL AT ONCE and kills your escape — that's the all-in. · Squads cross at grapple points. A defended rail caps how many of yours can fight at the foothold, so streams into a braced section get shredded. Local numbers win fights fast (and cheap). · MUSKETS: a section with a glint is loaded. When it levels (red flash) a volley is coming. After firing it shows a hollow ring — 6 seconds of open window. Time your streams into the window. · SWIVEL scatters a section for 3s (opens a window). MARKSMEN target a section and can drop its OFFICER (★) — officers stiffen morale and speed reloads. · Break a section's morale and it routs, panicking its neighbours. Cascades are how a smaller crew wins. · Take their QUARTERDECK and hold it to strike her colors. Lose your own, or lose your nerve, and the boarding is broken. They WILL cut idle grapples and counterattack.
