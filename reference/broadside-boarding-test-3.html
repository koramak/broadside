BOARDING — THE CHESSBOARD

Defenders
Your crew
HOW IT WORKS · Your pieces are squads. The number on each piece is its STRENGTH — its health AND its damage. A mauled squad hits soft. Melee damage = attacker strength minus defender armor (the small shield number). · Tap a piece, tap a blue tile to move (move is optional), then act: STRIKE an adjacent enemy, or use the piece's special. One move + one action each, then END TURN. · EVERY enemy shows its plan: an arrow to where it will move, and a red ✕ on the tile it will strike. The ✕ hits the TILE — step out of it, or shove someone else into it. · The dark water between the hulls kills anything that enters it. The BOSUN can SHOVE enemies one tile — into the gap, into each other, into their own volleys. · The CAPTAIN can RALLY: feed hands from your reserve pool into an adjacent squad. The MARINE shoots at range 3 (then reloads a turn). · Their tops fire a VOLLEY every third round — marked tiles burn anything standing there. Their hatch spawns reinforcements (telegraphed). Their OFFICER hardens nearby armor; drop him and watch them soften. · WIN: end a round standing on their colors (⚑) with no enemy beside you, or clear the deck. LOSE: lose every piece. CUT & RUN ends it any time — at a price.
